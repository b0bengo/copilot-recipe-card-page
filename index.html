Butter Chicken

A popular Indian dish made with tender Chicken cooked in a very creamy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:
  1. Chicken
  2. Butter
  3. Onion
  4. Garlic
  5. Ginger
  6. Tomatoes
  7. Heavy cream
  8. Spice (such as gram masala, tumeric, and chili powder)
  9. Salt
  10. Cliantro (for garnish)
Biryani

A flavour rice dish made with aromatic spices, meat (such as chicken, mutton or fish), and vegetables.

Ingredients:
  1. Rice
  2. Meat (chicken, mutton, or fish)
  3. Cumin
  4. Cucumber
  5. Tumeric
  6. Red chili powder
  7. Vegetables
Paneer Tikka

A vegeterian dish made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and grilled to perfection

Ingredients:
  1. Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Spices (such as cumin powder, coriander powder, tumeric and red chili powder)
  5. Lemon Juice
  6. Vegetables(such as bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes)
  7. Oil
  8. Salt
  9. Chaat masala (for garnish)
  10. Fresh coriander (for garnish)
